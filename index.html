🎯 GoNu Game Rules

🎮 Game Objective

The goal is to make it impossible for your opponent to move any of their pieces.

⚙️ Game Setup

  • Each player has 3 pieces
  • Blue pieces are placed at the top, red pieces at the bottom
  • Blue always goes first

🚀 Movement Rules

  • Pieces can only move along connected lines
  • Only one space per move
  • Cannot move to a space occupied by another piece
  • Diagonal moves are only allowed within the circle
  • Cannot retreat backwards within your own home area

🏆 Victory Conditions

  • Win when your opponent cannot make any moves
  • Lose when all your pieces are blocked or have no valid moves

🤖 AI Mode

The AI uses optimal strategy, calculating 6 moves ahead to choose the best possible move.
